Company Overview

Swift Clifts Afrique is a specialized due diligence firm dedicated to safeguarding mineral transactions across Africa. We are committed to transparency, compliance, and security, ensuring that every client makes informed decisions.

Mission Statement

“To safeguard the integrity of mineral trading through trusted due diligence, compliance solutions, and advisory services that protect our clients’ investments.”

Vision Statement

“To be Africa’s most trusted partner in due diligence and compliance for mineral transactions, setting global standards of excellence.”

Our Core Approach

We combine legal expertise, risk intelligence, and market insights to create a holistic due diligence process. By doing so, we provide clients with more than just verification – we deliver confidence, security, and peace of mind.
