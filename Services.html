What We Do

Swift Clifts Afrique offers a comprehensive range of due diligence and advisory services designed to protect clients engaged in mineral transactions. Our work ensures that buyers, sellers, and investors are safeguarded from fraud, regulatory issues, and reputational damage.

We deliver actionable intelligence, clear legal guidance, and trusted verification to support confident and profitable decision-making.

Due Diligence & Verification

We provide thorough due diligence investigations on mineral traders and companies before any business engagement. This process involves conducting comprehensive background checks, verifying identities, and analyzing business reputations across different markets. Our team compiles detailed due diligence reports that highlight the credibility, history, and integrity of potential partners. By uncovering hidden risks and validating the legitimacy of traders, we help clients avoid costly mistakes and establish partnerships with only genuine and trustworthy entities.

Company & Transaction Verification

Our verification services go beyond basic checks. We validate company registrations, licenses, ownership structures, and compliance with mining regulations in relevant jurisdictions. Additionally, we examine past business transactions and trading records to evaluate consistency and reliability. Fraud indicators, inconsistencies, or high-risk red flags are identified early, empowering our clients to make decisions with confidence. This ensures that every transaction is conducted with partners who are authentic, compliant, and transparent in their dealings.

Risk Intelligence & Compliance

We take a proactive approach to risk identification and management. Our compliance experts assess legal and regulatory gaps in proposed transactions, highlighting areas where exposure may exist. We also examine vulnerabilities to financial crimes such as money laundering, corruption, or illegal mineral sourcing. By addressing political, reputational, and operational risks in advance, we protect our clients from unexpected challenges, legal disputes, and potential disruptions to their businesses.

Financial Crime & Anti-Fraud Checks

The mineral trade is often targeted by fraudulent schemes and financial crimes. We deploy advanced tools to detect suspicious activities, enforce anti-money laundering (AML) compliance, and identify fraudulent practices. Our specialized fraud-check systems help trace inconsistencies in documents, verify trade authenticity, and ensure compliance with both local and international anti-crime regulations. This layer of protection safeguards investors and traders from reputational damage and financial losses linked to illicit dealings.

Market & Transaction Advisory

The mineral market, particularly in gold and rare earths, is highly dynamic and can shift rapidly. Our advisory team provides clients with insights into global market trends, demand forecasts, and price evaluations. We analyze trade patterns and transaction structures to recommend safe, efficient, and profitable business strategies. Clients benefit from timely and practical advice that helps them negotiate deals, structure transactions securely, and maximize returns while minimizing exposure to volatility and risks.

Legal Advisory & Support

Our legal team specializes in bridging the gap between mineral trade operations and compliance requirements. We offer end-to-end legal support including contract drafting and review to ensure fairness and enforceability. Additionally, we guide clients in meeting both local and international mining laws, while implementing anti-fraud and AML frameworks. In case of disputes, we provide negotiation, mediation, or litigation support. We also secure intellectual property and ownership rights, ensuring clients retain control over their mineral projects and assets.

Cross-Border Trade & Regulatory Compliance

International mineral transactions involve complex regulations, and non-compliance can result in severe financial and legal consequences. We assist clients in navigating diverse mining laws, export requirements, and trade restrictions across multiple jurisdictions. By offering clear compliance roadmaps, we eliminate uncertainties and ensure smooth, uninterrupted operations. Whether clients are buying, selling, or investing across borders, our expertise ensures transactions remain fully compliant, reducing exposure to costly penalties or delays.

ESG & Ethical Standards

We recognize the broader social and environmental impact of mineral trade. Our approach ensures that every transaction aligns with environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles. This includes encouraging eco-friendly mining practices that minimize ecological damage, supporting fair labor and community development, and ensuring transparent governance across operations. By integrating ethical standards into the due diligence process, we help clients build reputations as responsible industry leaders while safeguarding communities and preserving the environment.
